The Mouzy’s Story

Our journey began in 1985,in the group name of SHIMLA JUICE SHOP which was initiated by our beloved father.Back then we were a single shop situated in the trade market of Perinthalmanna Our father's primary goal of that venture was to deliver quality drinks with hygiene at affordable price. In 2001,we introduced Avilmilk in Shimla Juice shop.It received a lot of acceptance from food lovers.Since then through numerous trials and errors we shaped a unique brand for a single drink, Avil milk. It was the birth of MOUZY on March 2020 in Perinthalmanna.
